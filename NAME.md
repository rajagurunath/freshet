# Name: Freshet

**Freshet** *(noun, /ˈfreSHit/)* — a sudden rise of fresh water that swells a stream and feeds the river, fed by rain or snowmelt.

## Why this name
- **Relevant.** It extends the product's core thesis directly: every AI coding session is a *freshet* — a fresh flow of context — pouring into the company's larger river of knowledge that the one org-wide agent draws from. (Echoes the "company-wide agent, under the river" idea this product is built on.)
- **Unique / ownable.** A rare real word with essentially no collisions in the AI / developer-tools space — unlike *Tributary* (already an agentic-AI consultancy) and *Headwaters* (crowded with IT/VC/consulting firms). Strong shot at a clean domain and handle (e.g. `freshet.ai`, `freshet.dev`, `@freshet`).
- **Brandable.** Short, two syllables, easy to say and spell, works as a company name and a verb-ish product noun ("it's in Freshet").

## Tagline candidates
- *Every AI session, flowing into your company's memory.*
- *The freshet of context that feeds your team's knowledge.*

## Fit with the protocol
The session-exchange protocol stays **AICP (AI Context Protocol)** — Freshet is the product/company; AICP is the open standard it speaks.

## Ownable backups (same river/memory themes)
- **Rivulet** — a small stream feeding the river; same metaphor, very ownable.
- **Engram** — a stored memory trace (what a captured session literally is); memory-themed rather than river-themed.
